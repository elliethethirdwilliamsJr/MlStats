About MLSTATS

MLSTATS is a sleek, community-driven tracker built for Mobile Legends: Bang Bang fans and data geeks. It’s designed to give players a deeper look into hero statistics, match insights, and performance trends — all in one place.

Whether you're grinding to Mythic, researching optimal team picks, or just flexing your favorite hero’s stats, MLSTATS gives you a clear, responsive, and mobile-friendly interface to explore the world of MLBB analytics.

The tracker was handcrafted by Ellie the Dev out of pure passion for data visualization and the MLBB community. Every feature is built with the player in mind — fast loading, beautiful layouts, and useful data at your fingertips.
